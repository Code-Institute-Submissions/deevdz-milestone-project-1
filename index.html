Welcome to Headford Wellbeing Centre

Where we pride ourselves in offering the largest variety of classes found in any Yoga/Pilates studio in the West of Ireland. Ranging from strong classes like Hot Yoga, Vinyasa Flow Yoga, Yogalates, Pilates to more gentle classes like Gentle Yoga, Yoga Therapy for Back, Neck and Shoulder Problems, Pregnancy Yoga and Mindfulness Meditation. We have classes suitable for men and woman of all ages. If you prefer a more personal approach we also specialize in Personal Training.
